A Decade of Harmonious Excellence

Founded in 2014 by four friends at the Royal Academy of Music, Elysian Strings was born from a shared desire to deliver concert-level performances to unconventional spaces and intimate gatherings.

The Collective Spirit

Beyond Individual Brilliance

Elysian Strings is more than the sum of its four distinct voices. While each member brings a background of international solo excellence and orchestral leadership, our true identity is found in the unspoken synergy that only a decade of shared performance can forge. We believe that classical music is a living, breathing conversation—one that requires not just technical precision, but a profound emotional connection between every player.

From the first rehearsal at the Royal Academy to the grandest stages of London and beyond, our mission has remained unchanged: to bridge the gap between historical tradition and modern sensory experiences. We approach every arrangement with a commitment to acoustic purity, ensuring that whether we are performing a Vivaldi masterpiece or a cinematic contemporary score, the soul of the quartet remains the grounding force of every note.

The Craftsmanship

Voices from the 18th Century

The sound of Elysian Strings is not merely a product of technical mastery, but of the historical legacy held within our hands. Collectively, our ensemble performs on an extraordinary collection of Italian and French instruments, some dating back over 250 years.

From the resonant, dark textures of Julian’s 1782 Testore viola to the brilliant, vocal clarity of Elena’s Gagliano violin, these instruments are living artifacts. They require a unique dialogue—a sensitive understanding of how centuries-old wood responds to modern acoustics.

We believe that when you hire Elysian Strings, you are not just booking a performance; you are inviting the echoes of European history into your space.
